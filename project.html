Development of prototypes for teaching math to the visually impaired Public notice no 04/2020 - PROPI/RE/IFRN - Research and Innovation Projects with Development - Research Notice The advancement of technology has allowed the creation of increasingly sophisticated electronic devices. In parallel and also asa result of this progress, knowledge has become increasingly widespreadand a reflection of this reality is the maker culture and the "Do ItYourself" movement. These movements propagated various ideas andtechnologies, such as 3D printing and the use of computer numerical controlrouters. In an organic way, these devices were applied to the classroom context,seeking to mitigate learning difficulties and enable the integrationand development of students. This project seeks to develop devices thathelp teaching math to students with visual impairments or low vision. Wehope to have as results, devices, scripts and lesson plans. In case of apositive result in the validation stage, it is expected to produce kits anddonate to municipal schools in the municipality of Ipanguaçu.
