Welcome

Maker ip is the newest Laboratory Maker that makes up the range of laboratories at the IFRN Campus Ipanguaçu. To help spread this news, we have this website, which aims to provide students with any relevant information on the subject.

But what is a Lab. Maker? Lab. Maker is an area of ​​innovation and creation, within educational centers (this includes universities), focused on students transforming theory into practice.

What are we and where do we stay? Well, we are normal students and teachers... we created this page (without any kind of profit) to show our ideas, encounters, mistakes and successes, creations, evolutions etc.

The place where our Lab is located is at the Federal Institute of Education, Science and Technology of Rio Grande do Norte, Campus Ipanguaçu... Or simply: IFRN Campus Ipanguaçu. It is a 10/10 public educational institution!
